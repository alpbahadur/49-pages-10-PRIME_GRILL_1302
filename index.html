Born in Condado

PRIME GRILL 1302 opened its doors in the vibrant Condado neighborhood with a simple vision: to create a steakhouse that combines world-class quality with the warmth of family dining.

Our location at 1302 became more than just an address‚Äîit represents our commitment to being a cornerstone of the community, where locals and visitors alike can enjoy exceptional food in an atmosphere that feels like a second home.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we've built relationships with the finest local suppliers across Puerto Rico, ensuring that every dish showcases the island's incredible bounty.

From our signature prime steaks to our fresh seafood, paellas, and handcrafted pastas, every item on our menu is prepared with care, creativity, and a deep respect for culinary tradition.
